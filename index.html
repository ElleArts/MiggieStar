Miggie Star is a passionate singer known for her captivating voice and heartfelt performances. Blending elements of AfroBeat, AfroPop, Miggie’s music resonates with listeners, creating an emotional connection that lingers long after the final note. Other names : Big Miggie • Juju Girl.
Hailing from Abia State, Big Miggie discovered her passion for music at a young age. Growing up, she was inspired by legendary artists like Asa , Yemi Alade, TuBaba, whose powerful lyrics and melodies influenced her unique sound. With a natural talent for storytelling through songs, Miggie began performing in local venues, quickly gaining recognition for her distinctive style and vocal prowess.

Over the years, Big Miggie has honed her craft, captivating audiences with her powerful voice and emotional depth. Whether she’s performing an original composition or reinterpreting a classic hit, Big Miggie’s performances are always a journey through the highs and lows of life, love, and everything in between.
Now, with a growing fan base and a collection of original songs, Big Miggie is poised to make her mark on the music industry. She continues to write and perform music that speaks to the heart, sharing her journey and inspiring others through the universal language of song.
